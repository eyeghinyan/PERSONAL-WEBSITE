About Me

Hello! I'm Erik, a passionate traveler and advocate for affordable exploration through the Erasmus+ program. My goal is to help others discover the world on a budget and make meaningful connections through travel.

As a dedicated traveler, I believe in the power of travel to open minds and reshape perspectives. My mission is to empower others to embark on their adventures, uncover new cultures, and forge meaningful connections—all while navigating the journey on a budget.

Through my platform, "Wheels of Discovery," I provide insightful tips, compelling stories, and valuable resources to inspire you to embrace the opportunities that Erasmus+ presents. Whether you're considering a volunteering project, a study exchange, or a youth training course, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

Join me in celebrating the beauty of cultural exchange as we discover the world together and become change-makers in our communities. The journey awaits—let's embark on it together!

My First Erasmus Project

My first Erasmus project took place in Kaunas, where I volunteered as an English teacher for children. This experience was transformative, both for the students and for me. I was motivated to join this project because I wanted to contribute to the community and help young minds learn a new language.

Getting involved was a simple yet rewarding process. I applied through a local organization, and after some interviews and training, I was on my way to Kaunas. The joy of seeing the kids grow in their language skills was unmatched and inspired me to continue engaging in similar projects.

This experience not only enhanced my teaching skills but also deepened my appreciation for different cultures and the power of education. I learned to adapt my teaching style to meet the needs of diverse learners, fostering a supportive and engaging environment.
